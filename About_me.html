About Me

Hello, my name is Caleb McClain and I'm a graphic designer. I'm currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Technical Communication, and I have extensive experience working with Adobe Creative Cloud applications, such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

As a designer, I'm passionate about creating unique and visually appealing designs that meet the needs of my clients. I have honed my skills by working on a variety of projects, including logo design, branding, print materials, and digital media.

I also have a strong background in running a promotional products company. This experience has given me a deep understanding of the complete process of taking digital designs and turning them into physical products. I'm well-versed in the various techniques and materials used in the production of promotional products, such as screen printing, embroidery, and sublimation. My experience has taught me how to create designs that are both visually appealing and practical, ensuring that they look great on a variety of different products. As a designer, I'm passionate about creating unique designs that meet the needs of my clients. Whether you're looking for a new logo, branding, print materials, or digital media, I have the skills and expertise to bring your vision to life. I'm also able to work well in a team environment and have excellent interpersonal communication skills.
