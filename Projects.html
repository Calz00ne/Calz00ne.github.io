Space Walk

Space Walk Vol.1 is a fictional science fiction manga that I have created to illustrate my ability to render images with real weathering effects. I really enjoy the process of adding elements to my images to make them feel as though they are photographs of real world objects. I created this image in Adobe Photoshop using multiple tools such as gradient maps, masks, shape tools, text tools, and image adjustment layers. The weather effects were created using external elements and specific blending settings to create a natural and realistic view.
